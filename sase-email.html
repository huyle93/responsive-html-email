More than an Orgnization, we are a big family #SASEFAM

"About a year ago, four crazy people (Novina, Huy, Thanh, and I) at UNH had the idea to empower the Pan-Asian community by creating an organization that provides opportunities for professional development, academic success, community outreach, and cultural celebration. It has been such an honor to serve as the first President and a founder for this incredible organization. SASE has given me opportunities to travel to incredible conferences in Houston and Boston, learn from and mentor an amazing group of people, travel to middle schools and impact the future of STEM, and most importantly create a lot of wonderful memories. I've been so inspired by the members of this org and seeing so many of you at the First Annual Gala meant the world to me.
So thank you SASE fam, I love you all and I can't wait to see where this organization goes! Bright future ahead for SASE!" - Sid Nigam, UNH SASE Former President
